FAQ · Terms · Common Questions
❓ What is Magic ChromoWords?
🇺🇸

Magic ChromoWords™ is an artistic system that transforms names and words into symbolic artworks using a rainbow color-sequencing method, where each letter's color and position carry meaning.

❓ Is every artwork unique?
🇺🇸

Yes. Each piece is individually hand-crafted by the author. No two compositions are identical, even when the same word is requested twice.

❓ How long does delivery take?
🇺🇸

Your finished artwork is delivered to your email within 72 hours of purchase.

❓ Can I choose colors?
🇺🇸

At Level III — Premium Collaboration, you can choose the background color and the color of each individual letter. At Levels I and II, color is determined by the artist.

❓ Can I choose a font?
🇺🇸

Yes, starting from Level II — Font Choice, you can select from our curated catalogue of 20 fonts. All fonts used are free for personal and commercial use.

❓ What is included?
🇺🇸

Every commission includes 1 final artwork, a high-resolution file, and a Magic ChromoWords™ Certificate of Authenticity.

❓ Do I receive commercial rights?
🇺🇸

No. Purchasing a commission grants a license for personal use only. Commercial use requires a separate commercial license. Exclusive rights may be purchased separately. Copyright always remains with the author.
